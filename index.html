About Me

I'm an SFU student in the School of Interactive Arts & Technology, where I blend technical craftsmanship with creative problem-solving. My work lives at the intersection of 3D space and the web.

I use tools like Rhino 8, Maya, and Onshape to design and animate detailed 3D models, and I bring those ideas onto the screen with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I'm drawn to projects that ask both how does this work and how does this feel — from a precisely jointed pavilion structure to a smoothly looping underwater animation.
